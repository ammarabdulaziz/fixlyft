FAQ

  What is Fixlyft?

Fixlyft is India's first and most trusted online gadget only repair portal connecting customers with their preferred service centers for premium quality care. Fixlyft also offers free pick-up of your device from your location and delivery after completion of service

  Is my gadget safe with Fixlyft?

Fixlyft ensures the safety of your electronic devices by providing undertaking responsibility against any possible theft/loss caused while the device is in our possession. Your data & privacy is safe with us.

How do I schedule a pickup with Fixlyft?

You can schedule a pickup just by filling a form in 'schedule pickup' section. We will contact you.

How does Fixlyft work?

With Fixlyft, now you can repair any device at any service center of your choice all at the comfort of your home by selecting your preferred service point and scheduling a pick-up for your device.

What time does it take for a gadget to be repaired & delivered?

Fixlyft always strives to provide quick service for your devices. However,the actual time of repair for your devices may vary according to the service needed to be done. All updates are given to the customer on real-time basis.

What happens to my device/devices once it is picked up?

You will receive an SMS & email confirmation once your gadget has been picked-up. Your gadget is then dispatched to your pre-selected service center/point where it is diagnosed and repaired.
All real-time updates are sent to the customer through email and SMS.

Is my personal information safe and private?

Customer privacy is of the utmost concern to us. Our employees and partners are refrained from accessing into your device. No data is accessed or looked into at any point of time between pick-up to delivery.

What gadgets can i repair using Fixlyft?

With Fixlyft, you can easily repair/maintain your laptops, smartphones, gaming consoles and tablets.

How to select a service center of my choice?

Our partners are listed on our site and there are options to contact them and to schedule a pick-up for your devices.
You can select from among the best shops in your area based on real customer ratings.
